             at pixeljoint.com. I seriously suggest going to the site and browsing
                             the galleries.                             

           In order to respect the artists and make it known that it is not my work           
            I am distributing this file along with 'kz.png' I will list the artists
            of the work that appears in the file in order from left to right, then

            the FULL artwork that was used. Because of the size constraints in the
           template I had to crop A LOT of the actual pixel art. You'd be surprised
               how much is actually missing. Most of the smallest paintings are
             actually very large pieces of pixel art. I recommend checking out the
           full piece to get the most experience from these awesome pieces of pixel
                                            work.
